The Beatles:

World building for the world's biggest band.

Having successfully pitched the idea to Apple Corps, I oversaw the creative for a series of animations designed to capture the essence of each song on the 2022 re-release of Revolver. For three months leading up to release, I consulted with producers from Apple Corps and archivists from Abbey Road, directed a team of animators from the production company WILD, and liaised with domestic/international marketing teams across Universal to deliver each animation to respective territories. Working with these teams was a remarkable experience that lead to a Music Week Award nomination for Best Catalogue Campaign and a seal of approval from Paul McCartney.
