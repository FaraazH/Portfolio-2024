Chromatic:

Music theory for non-musicians.

For professional musicians, a principled understanding of music theory underpins their ability to read, write, and perform music. For those at the start of their journey however, this comes with a steep learning curve that poses a significant barrier to entry. Despite the vast amounts of information on the subject, music theory is field steeped in tradition and designed with the initiated in mind. For those looking to learn an instrument without undergoing classical training, this makes navigating even the most basic concepts particularly challenging. The below is an exploration into how we might design a system that fosters a greater sense of understanding among the newcomers to the field, without undermining the language of music.
