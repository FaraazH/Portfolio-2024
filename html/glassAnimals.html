Glass Animals: Stepping inside the album cover.

Charged with revitalising the Glass Animals catalogue from a visual standpoint, I worked alongside the campaign team at Universal to bring audiences inside the surreal and tropical world of their debut album. Having initially redesigned the album artwork in 3D, it quickly became apparent that the scene could be explored in a virtual setting. While the idea was simple to pitch, designing the experience brought about its own technical challenges. Working closely with a 3D designer, we built three separate scenes inspired by the album artwork.
