Glass Animals: Stepping inside the album cover.

Charged with revitalising the Glass Animals catalogue from a visual standpoint, I worked alongside the campaign team at Universal to bring audiences inside the surreal and tropical world of their debut album, Zaba. Having redesigned the album artwork in 3D, it became apparent that the scene could be further explored in a virtual setting. While the band were on board with the idea, designing the experience brought about its own technical challenges. Working closely with a 3D designer, we built three separate scenes inspired by the album artwork.
