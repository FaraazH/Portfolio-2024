Yellow Submarine:

Designing listening experiences.

With the entire history of recorded music now available at our fingertips, it may come as little surprise that consumption of catalogue music has superseded consumption of new releases in recent years. Artists of the 20th century have seen continued resurgence through strategic syncs and UGC, proving that it’s less about when a song was released and more about the context in which it’s presented. Finding new ways for audiences to experience their favourite artist, album, or song has the potential to create considerable value for existing fans and new-listeners alike. This project seeks to explore this idea by considering how we might design a listening experience for John Lennon fans passing from Tottenham Court Road to the Outernet, using his demo of Yellow Submarine as a source of inspiration.
